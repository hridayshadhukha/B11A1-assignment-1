Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating new ideas, methods, or products that are valuable to customers and stakeholders.

Sustainability

Sustainability is the practice of using resources responsibly so they can be maintained for the long term.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is the basic facilities and systems serving a country, city, or area, such as transportation, power plants, and schools.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of giving people the tools and confidence to make decisions and achieve goals.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nations trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens..

Green Energy

Green energy is energy that comes from renewable resources and doesn't harm the environment.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
